About Me

My life In Shanghai

For the last 12 years I have been living abroad in Shanghai, China. This surprises a lot of people, and as a result I get a lot of questions like, "do you speak chinese?" or, "but you dont look chinese...?" But yes, I did live in Shanghai for over a decade, and it was absolutely amazing. In Shanghai I was able to meet so many people from all sorts of backgrounds from all over the world. I also was able to get plugged in to the local music scene and play a bunch of gigs. In Shanghai I met a lot of lifelong friends, and had many incredible experiences, like hiking through the mountains north of BeiJing, helping install a water system in YunNan, and getting to eat JianBing for breakfast cooked by a local street vendor on my way to school every morning. Living in an Shanghai greatly shaped who I am as a person and gave me a greater global perspective. If anyone is planning on visiting Asia, I would definitely recommend visiting Shanghai along the way!

Me now

Currently I am in college at the University of Texas at Austin, and I could not love it more. I am a Computer Science major, and am currently taking Data Structures and Discrete Math (in addition to some other core classes). I have also had a blast getting involved in student organizations on campus, such as Texas Wake, Longhorn Swim Club, Collegium Musicum Choir, and EGaDS! (Electronic Game Developers Society). The people I have met here so far have made college alright alright alright. Hook 'em!
